About Me
Hello! I'm Manny, a sophomore at UC Berkeley studying Mechanical Engineering. I'm from Simi Valley, and have lived there my full life. I am interested in Robotics, and am currently trying to seek out research in the field. I spent the summer taking summer classes and learning raspberry pi to make myself more marketable for robotics research. I am currently taking CS61A, trying out computer science for a fit. Hoping for the best! I like to play soccer in my free time, and am currently playing intamural indoor and outdoor soccer. I also am on CalSol, UC Berkeley's solar vehicle team. I participated in the American Solar Challenge this summer, which was a cross country race from Ohio to South Dakata (2000 miles) all with our solar car. Despite the rainy conditions, our car placed 9th out of the 22 teams from across the US, Canada, and even Europe! I am excited to see the team's hard work pay off, and I cant wait till next race!
